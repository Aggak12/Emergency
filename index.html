Creators of VELVET dating app

The Soviet Dolls’ compatibility ranking system reshapes modern dating by eliminating imbalance and guesswork.

The Soviet Dolls have officially entered the dating market with VELVET, a platform built on one simple idea:
You date at your level.

Unlike traditional apps that rely mostly on photos and random swipes, VELVET evaluates the entire profile. The Dolls scan and review:
- Photos
- Hobbies & interests
- Carreer path
- Financial status
- Communication style
- Core values
Each user is assigned a tier. You can only see and match with people in your tier. No exceptions.

The system removes what the Dolls call “fantasy matching” — the common frustration of chasing profiles far outside one’s actual effort or compatibility level. Users who improve their profiles — clearer intentions, better photos, stronger initiative — can request reevaluation. Promotions are possible. However, according to insiders, the Dolls’ “no-BS meter” is highly calibrated.

Early analytics show the platform is now one of the fastest-growing dating apps in Denmark. Internal surveys report:
- Higher match satisfaction
- Fewer ghosting complaints
- Lower mismatch rates
- Increased profile effort across all tiers
While some users admit that receiving a lower rank can be uncomfortable, many say it acts as motivation.
One user shared:
“At first I was offended. Then I rewrote my profile, updated my photos, and actually put effort in. I got promoted two tiers. It worked.”
